Emergency Helpline numbers

             Emergency helplines serve as critical lifelines for individuals facing immediate threats, including sexual harassment. These helplines are designed to provide 24/7 confidential support, guidance, and assistance to victims in distress. When someone calls an emergency helpline, trained professionals or counselors respond promptly, offering a safe space to discuss their situation without fear of judgment. Depending on the nature of the issue, helplines can connect callers to law enforcement, medical assistance, or psychological support. Many also offer actionable advice on how to handle harassment, access legal aid, and ensure personal safety. Some helplines provide multilingual support, anonymous reporting options, and follow-up services to help victims regain control and stability in their lives. By bridging the gap between victims and critical resources, these services play a vital role in ensuring safety, justice, and recovery.
