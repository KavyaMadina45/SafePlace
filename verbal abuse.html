Verbal abuse

                   Verbal abuse is a harmful form of emotional and psychological harm where words are used to manipulate, control, or hurt another person. It can occur in personal relationships, workplaces, schools, or online, often taking forms like name-calling, criticism, threats, or gaslighting. The effects of verbal abuse are significant, leading to low self-esteem, anxiety, depression, and strained social relationships. It may also indirectly cause physical issues like sleep disturbances or weakened immunity. Recognizing abuse, setting boundaries, seeking support, and prioritizing self-care are essential steps in addressing it. Providing empathy and encouraging professional help can also make a difference for those experiencing verbal abuse.
